# **App Name**: Panetone Gourmet da May

## Core Features:

- Gourmet Panetone Recipes: Provides detailed recipes and techniques for creating gourmet panetones with premium fillings.
- Step-by-Step Video Tutorials: Offers video tutorials demonstrating each step of the panetone-making process, from dough preparation to filling and finishing.
- Premium Filling Combinations: Suggests unique and decadent filling combinations to elevate the panetones to a gourmet level.
- Bonus Module: Selling and Profiting: Includes a bonus module with tips and strategies on how to sell and profit from the gourmet panetones during the Christmas season.
- AI-Powered Flavor Suggestions: Tool to suggest innovative flavor combinations based on user preferences and available ingredients.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep red (#8B0000) to evoke the rich, warm feelings of Christmas.
- Background color: Off-white (#FAF9F6) for a clean and gourmet aesthetic.
- Accent color: Gold (#FFD700) to represent the premium and luxurious nature of the panetones.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', serif, for a sophisticated and elegant feel, in keeping with the 'high-end' feel suggested by the user. Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, in keeping with the user request for a modern feel.
- Modern icons representing the different steps of the panetone-making process, the ingredients and tools, and the selling tips.
- Visually-driven layout with high-quality photos and videos showcasing the texture, brightness, and creaminess of the panetones. Cards with premium aesthetics.
- Subtle animations and transitions to create a modern and sophisticated user experience, without being too distracting.
